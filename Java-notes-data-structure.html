<h1 id="data-structures">Data Structures</h1>
<h2 id="linked-list">Linked List</h2>
<h3 id="properties">Properties</h3>
<p>A linked list</p>
<ul>
<li>consists of a group of nodes in sequence</li>
<li>grows and shrinks dynamically</li>
<li>is made up of two parts: data and reference to the next node</li>
</ul>
<p><code>Head</code>: first node<br>
<code>Tail</code>: last node</p>
